How We Moved Away from Spryker to Symfony (and Deleted 2M Lines of Code)

Spryker is an e-commerce platform particularly popular in Germany. At Sourceability, we have encountered the limitations of Spryker and made the decision to migrate away from it, embracing the Symfony full-stack framework.

Symfony User Group Berlin -Berlin, Germany
2023-07-05

Spryker is an e-commerce platform particularly popular in Germany. At Sourceability, we have encountered the limitations of Spryker and made the decision to migrate away from it, embracing the Symfony full-stack framework.

In this talk, I will share their journey of migrating from Spryker to Symfony and the challenges they faced along the way. O will discuss the strategies we have employed to successfully transition our codebase and how we managed to remove a staggering 2 million lines of code.

If yuu are interested to know more on how we did it, do not hesitate to contact me.
