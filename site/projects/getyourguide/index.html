GetYourGuide

Online marketplace for travel activities.

Role: software engineer

GetYourGuide is a leading global online marketplace that enables travelers to discover and book a wide array of travel experiences, including tours, excursions, and tickets to attractions. Founded in 2009 by a group of students from the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich, the company is headquartered in Berlin, Germany, and operates worldwide.

The platform offers over 100,000 activities across more than 12,000 cities globally, partnering with over 20,000 suppliers. These experiences range from guided city tours and museum visits to adventure activities and cultural experiences. getyourguide.press+1Wikipedia+1

GetYourGuide operates by connecting travelers with local tour operators, taking a commission on each booking. The company also curates its own branded experiences under the "Originals by GetYourGuide" label, ensuring high-quality standards.

With a user-friendly website and mobile app, GetYourGuide provides flexible booking options, including free cancellation up to 24 hours in advance and 24/7 multilingual customer support, enhancing the travel planning experience for users worldwide.
